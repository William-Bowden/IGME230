Genre

Horizontal Dodging/Vertical Shooter

It will play a bit like Space Invaders in the sense that you can move horizontally, dodging incoming projectiles/objects while you are also able to shoot back at them as they approach.

Platform

Desktop Browser

Currently, it is only planned to be able to be played within a desktop browser via keyboard input.

Story

In this intense, fast-paced 2D-shooter you need to fight your way to freedom in a seemingly endless wave of enemies, known as The Sprites. The Sprites are evil creatures that want to keep you from getting to your destination; "The End".

Esthetics

Simple sprite based Art and 8-bit audio tracks
