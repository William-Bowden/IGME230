Tower Defense

Tower Defense is a game I made while teaching myself the ropes of the Unity3D engine. It is a simple Tower Defense game where you earn currency by killing enemies to purchase and place turrets to help further defend the path to your base from the incoming waves of enemies.

Nav Mesh Demo

This footage is from a demo I made when teaching myself how to implement Unity2D's nav meshes with a simple point and click movement system. I also learned how the Character Controller that belongs to the Unity Standard Assets package vastly simplifies movement animation on humanoid models.

Asteroids

Over the course of a couple of days I created Asteroids, which was a game directly influenced by the original Asteroids. It was, yet again, a learning experience because it was the first time I have ever worked with the concept of object pooling.

Quote Matcher

Quote Matcher: The Office is a simple DOM game made with HTML, CSS, and Javascript by another student and I where you are given random character quotes from the show The Office and are asked to guess who said that quote.
