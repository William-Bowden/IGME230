Genre

Single-Room 2D Platformer

In Blobby, you play as an energetic little blob who loves to adventure, even when the spaces available are much too small for his energy level. Guide Blobby

Platform

Desktop Browser

Currently, it is only planned to be able to be played within a desktop browser with keyboard input.

  • W - Jump
  • A/D - Move Blobby in that direction (A = left, D = right)
  • S - Drop down through a blue platform

Esthetics

Mostly Pixi shapes representing game assets, but there are also simple sprite based Art and custom audio tracks
