Welcome

Blobby is a game in which you bounce around to different platforms within a room in an effort to reach the room key so you can move on to the next room. Rooms are randomly generated, so there's always something new waiting for you. Watch out, you may encounter some traps along the way!

Instructions

What you will encounter

You guide Blobby throughout a room in an attempt to get the key to the next room. This can be done by jumping Blobby around the room, utilizing platforms and walls along the way. The main difference between these two objects are that you can not go through walls, but you can go through platforms. Avoid hazards along your way. They will hurt Blobby and set him back to the start. Blobby has three lives, see how far you can get!

Controls

  • W - Jump
  • A/D - Move Blobby in that direction (A = left, D = right)
  • S - Drop down through a blue platform
